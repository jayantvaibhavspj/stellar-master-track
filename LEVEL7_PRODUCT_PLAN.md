# Level 7 Product Plan

## Product Concept
**StellarFlow Mainnet** is the next evolution of your Stellar challenge journey. It combines your earlier crowdfunding work with advanced real-time payment streaming and mainnet-ready launch strategy.

## Why this product?
Your previous belt projects show a clear progression:
- **Yellow Belt**: Basic crowdfunding dApp on Testnet
- **Orange Belt**: Full deployment, tests, and product polish
- **Green Belt**: Production-ready crowdfunding with CI/CD
- **Black Belt**: Programmable payment streaming with advanced features

For Level 7, the goal is to turn these learnings into a sustainable startup product on Stellar Mainnet.

## Product Name
**StellarFlow Mainnet**

## Core value proposition
Enable creators, freelancers, and small teams to receive continuous payments in real time on Stellar Mainnet with low friction, clear analytics, and optional gasless onboarding.

## Target users
- Creators and subscription businesses
- Freelancers and contractors
- DAO or community treasury operators
- Small teams paying salaries or retainers

## Main problem solved
Traditional payment flows are slow, manual, and opaque. StellarFlow Mainnet makes payments:
- continuous instead of lump-sum
- transparent and auditable on-chain
- easy for users to start with wallet onboarding
- low-friction with optional fee sponsorship

## Key features
1. Wallet onboarding (Freighter, Lobstr, xBull)
2. Mainnet payment stream creation
3. Withdraw earned balance anytime
4. Cancel or pause streams
5. Stream templates for subscriptions and salaries
6. Stream analytics dashboard
7. Fee sponsorship / gasless transactions
8. User feedback collection and iteration
9. Community growth and social proof
10. Mainnet transaction and user proof

## Level 7 proof strategy
### Mainnet launch
- Deploy Soroban smart contracts on Stellar Mainnet
- Publish contract explorer links and deployment TX hashes

### User growth
- Onboard 50+ mainnet users with real wallets

### Feedback and product development
- Collect feedback with Google Form or spreadsheet
- Build feature improvements and commit them with clear messages
- Use `user_feedback_responses.csv` and `FEEDBACK_ANALYSIS.md`

### Marketing & community
- Publish at least 3 product update posts
- Share mainnet launch and progress on social channels
- Collect social proof with 50+ followers or engagement

## Product roadmap

### Phase 3: Growth & retention
